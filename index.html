Documentation

Understanding VeriMedia AI and interpreting your results.

Getting Started

VeriMedia AI is a multimodal tool designed to help you verify the authenticity of news and media on the internet.

  • Video Analysis: Upload a short clip or screenshot of a video to check for deepfake markers.
  • Article & Image: Paste a news headline or upload an article screenshot to evaluate journalistic integrity and visual manipulation.
  • Social Media: (Coming Soon) Paste a social URL to check for bot amplification.

Understanding the AI Verdict

After running an analysis, the system will output a Fraud Index (0-100%) and a verdict:

  • AUTHENTIC (0-15%): The content exhibits high journalistic standards and no signs of manipulation.
  • LOW RISK (16-30%): Minor sensationalism or minor clickbait, but fundamentally factual.
  • MEDIUM RISK (31-60%): Highly biased, misleading framing, or unverified claims. Approach with caution.
  • HIGH RISK (61-100%): Clear signs of fabrication, AI generation, deepfakes, or known conspiracy patterns.

Modality Scores Explained

The system breaks down its analysis into three independent pillars to ensure accuracy:

1. NLP (Textual Authenticity)

Analyzes the headline and any visible text for sensationalism, logical fallacies, and AI-generation patterns (e.g., uniform sentence structure common in LLMs).

2. CV (Computer Vision)

When an image is provided, the Vision AI scans for artifacting, inconsistent lighting, and unnatural blending which are hallmarks of manipulated media or AI generation.

3. Cross-Modal Logic

Checks for consistency between the text and the image. For example, if a headline claims "City on fire!" but the image shows a peaceful street, this score will flag a high mismatch.

Privacy & Security

Your API keys are stored strictly in your local .env file. Any media you upload is processed entirely locally and immediately deleted from the uploads/ directory the moment the analysis completes. We do not store your files.
